Privacy Policy — Social Media Automation App

Effective Date: 25-11-2025
This Privacy Policy explains how our Social Media Automation application (“we”, “our”, or “the service”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our platform to generate AI-powered content and publish to your social media accounts.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 User-Provided Information

Name

Email address

Login details for your account on our platform (NOT your social media passwords)

1.2 Social Media Authentication Data

When you connect your social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn, YouTube), we receive:

Access tokens

Refresh tokens

Platform-specific user IDs

Account username
We never collect or store your social media passwords.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

IP address

Browser type

Usage logs (errors, API calls, posting status)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

Authenticating your social media accounts using official OAuth login systems

Posting content on your behalf only after your approval

Generating captions, hashtags, and topics through AI (Gemini)

Providing relevant images using the Unsplash API

Saving your connected account status so you don’t have to log in every time

Improving app performance and troubleshooting issues

We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. How We Store and Protect Data

Tokens and credentials are stored securely in our database with encryption.

Sensitive keys are never visible to users or client-side scripts.

All communications are encrypted using HTTPS.

Access is restricted and monitored for security.

4. Posting on Your Behalf

We will only post content to platforms you have explicitly connected and selected during publishing.
You can disconnect access at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Graph APIs

X/Twitter API

LinkedIn API

YouTube/Google OAuth API

Unsplash API

Google Gemini AI API

Each service has its own privacy policy, which you agree to when authenticating your account.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Disconnect any social media account

Delete your account from our application

Request deletion of all stored data

Update your email or profile information

Revoke permissions from any connected platform

7. Data Retention

We store account details only as long as your account is active.
If you delete your account, all associated tokens and generated content will be removed.

8. Children’s Privacy

This service is not intended for users under the age of 13.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Continued use of the service means you accept any updates.
